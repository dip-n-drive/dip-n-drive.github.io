Our Story

How did we get here?



Sam LOVES ketchup and Sam LOVES sports!


So when his boys began travel ball and school sports, he and Sandy were on the road - A LOT. Fast food became an essential part of the travelling routine to be able to watch both boys' games.

Sam loves his fries with ketchup, but staying on the move made it hard to do. He would open multiple packets and try to dip his food in ketchup, and other condiments, usually making a mess while trying to keep his eyes on the road. Finally he came up with the perfect idea - and Dip N' Drive was born!
