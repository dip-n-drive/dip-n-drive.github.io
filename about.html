Our Story

How did we get here?

Sam LOVES ketchup and Sam LOVES sports!

So when his boys began travel ball and school sports, he and Sandy were on the road A LOT. Fast food became necessary evil to be able to watch both boys' games.
Sam loves dipping his fries into ketchup, but dipping and driving had not yet been perfected. Sam would have to rip open multiple packets and carefully squirt them into another container without making a mess. Unfortunately, this was only the beginning of the fiasco. Once on the road, Sam had to keep his eyes on the road while feeling around for the floating container of ketchup without knock over the container or accidentally dipping his finger into it.
Finally after spending 8 hours on the road every weekend, Sam perfected the Dip 'N Drive container as it is today.
