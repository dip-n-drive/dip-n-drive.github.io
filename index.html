FAQs

Q: Will the container sit flat on the table?

A: Yes - Dip 'N Drive containers are even STACKABLE!

Q: How many times do I use the container?

A: The containers are SINGLE-USE because they are pre-packaged and sealed with sauce.

Q: Can I still drink with an opened container on my cup?

A: Yes - sauces such as ketchup, BBQ, and ranch have a high viscosity and do NOT pour out when tilting a drink.
